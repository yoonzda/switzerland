Rocksresort

  • This extraordinary resort comprises eight modern buildings located at the valley station of the mountain railways in Laax and is a world apart: the resort has 186 stylishly and comfortably furnished rooms and apartments that can sleep two, four, six or eight people, a range of shops, bars and several restaurants. Whether you want fast food or gourmet delights in the restaurant, there’s something for everyone here. Ski-in ski-out; located directly on bike and hiking trails; large green areas; proudly carries the “Families welcome” logo.

  • Description

    The rocksresort is impressive with its unique design and regional materials. Experience comfortably equipped apartments for 2, 4, 6 or 8 people. At the rocksresort, alpine lifestyle is reinterpreted and celebrated with a passion. You will enjoy the highest standards of accommodation. There are 186 high-quality, fully furnished 4, 6 and 8-bed apartments available. The complex is located in an amazingly beautiful, unique mountain landscape. Besides the wide range of services, there is also a wealth of restaurants, bars and shops.
