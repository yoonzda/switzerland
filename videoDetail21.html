Donkey trekking with a view of the Churfirsten range

  • The first item on this special walk is “donkey knowledge” before everybody prepares their “own” donkey and sets out into the lovely Toggenburg landscape.

    During the donkey trekking through the Toggenburg landscape, animals and nature are experienced first-hand for a few hours or even for a whole day. The fun already begins in the stable, where everybody loads up their “own” donkey and learns all sorts of interesting things about the animals. People used to think they were stubborn, but nowadays the grazing “landscaper managers” are greatly appreciated. The highlight of the trek is to lead the animals through the beautiful outdoors and of course the view of the Toggenburg peaks.
